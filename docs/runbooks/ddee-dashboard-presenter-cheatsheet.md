# D-DEE Dashboard Presenter Cheatsheet

## One-Liner

This dashboard connects lead source, speed-to-lead, sales/revenue, retention,
and customer-level evidence so the business can see where money is being made or
lost.

## Demo Flow

1. **Speed-to-Lead**
   - Are leads being worked?
   - Who is reaching them?
   - Where are follow-up leaks?

2. **Lead Magnets**
   - Which magnets create buyers, not just leads?
   - What was the latest magnet before purchase?
   - Which sources need cleaner taxonomy?

3. **Revenue**
   - How much net revenue was collected?
   - What products/payment patterns are showing up?
   - Where is revenue credit strong or unknown?

4. **Retention**
   - Who needs payment recovery?
   - Which customers are due, failed, paid off, or upsell-ready?
   - Where do manual collections show up?

5. **Customer 360**
   - Click one customer.
   - Show the relationship timeline.
   - Show payments, magnet trail, retention state, and open actions.

## Say This

> The point is not just dashboards. The point is making the revenue system
> inspectable.

> We can move from a metric to the customer evidence behind it.

> We are careful about truth. If we do not know current ownership, we do not
> pretend to know it.

## Avoid Saying

- "This is a finished CRM."
- "This replaces your sales process."
- "Every owner is known."
- "Transcript amounts are contract truth."
- "The action queue is fully operational."

## Strongest Proof Points

- Live BigQuery freshness badges.
- Customer 360 clickthrough.
- Retention recovery queue.
- Revenue credit gap shown honestly.
- Relationship timeline connecting source to money.
